Socrates Uduk Features Jaywon In New Single by innamoni: 11:25am On Oct 30
News made the rounds recently that Socrates Uduk aka Soc’ was going to release a single that would feature a popular artist. The report had it that Socrates Uduk had the choice of collaborating with one among Jaywon, Skales, Shaydee and Patoranking.

It is confirmed that Socrates Uduk decided to go for Jaywon and that the song, produced by Drew, is titled, “Cure My Craze”.

It is confirmed that Jaywon was in the studio on the 28th October 2014 to voice his part in the song and Socrates Uduk and Owen Gee were at the studio with him while he voiced and that the song will be released in a few days time.

I was also able to confirm that it is a hip hop song with beats that is sure to get you dancing unstoppably Socrates Uduk took two verses in the song while Jaywon sang the chorus and the bridge.

This report has been further confirmed as Socrates Uduk on his twitter handle, "@socrates_uduk" tweeted "Ose Owen Gee, Jaywon and Drew. You guys rock", he also posted the same comment on his facebook music page named named "Socrates Uduk".

Jaywon whose full name is James Oluwajuwonlo Edahi is an award winning Nigerian musician and has tracks with Tiwa Savage, Goldie, Essence, Olamide, Terry G, Ice Prince, Vector etc.

Socrates Uduk who was Madonna University entertainment icon, was recently giving a louder voice and placed on a higher pedestal by award winning entertainer, Owen Gee.

Are we talking of a hit track in the making? Only time will tell. You can be sure to have the track on this site when it is released.
