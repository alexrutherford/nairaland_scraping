Job Details
Rockstone International Nigeria is recruiting to fill the position of:

Job Title: ICT Instructor/Technicians
Duties
• He/she will be responsible for system wide technical support to curriculum and administrative.
• In co-operation with teachers he/she will support the teaching of ICT by preparing relevant materials.
• Ensure that a supply of consumable items such as printer cartridges and toners are maintained, recorded and reordered as required.
• Providing help and guidance to pupils on an individual or group basis within ICT lessons.
• Work with teachers in the classroom to promote the use of ICT across the curriculum.

Requirements:
• First degree and/or teaching degree, OND, HND, (Engineering and Computer Sciences is an added advantage)
• Teaching experience in Computer Sciences is an would be an added advantage)
Method of Application:
Applications should consist of a covering letter of no more than 2 A4 sides and a current CV with photograph and sent to:

rockstoneinternationaljobs@gmail.com
