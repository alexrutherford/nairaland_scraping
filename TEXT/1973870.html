A Touching Letter From My Student by Vanellope(f): 11:56am On Oct 30
I got this letter from a girl I taught maths during the holidays. I just felt parents and students should do this more often.

The letter reads...

"While I was writing this letter, I was in tears, the reason is because I haven't found a mathematics teacher as best as you are, and I just wish I could have someone like you as my teacher. But I am not happy that you were unable to do much teachings with me. I just hope that by the virtue that you thought (sic) me, God will see that to make me do well in my WAEC. Thank you very much sir for your care. You are one in a million. i really appreciate your effort. It was nice meeting with you. Here is my number... Thank you and God bless."
